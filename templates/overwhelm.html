General Overwhelm

"You don’t have to fix everything today."

General overwhelm happens when your brain is juggling too many responsibilities, decisions, and expectations at once. It’s not laziness. It’s cognitive overload.

Why It Feels Paralyzing

When tasks stack up, your brain shifts into threat mode. Instead of prioritizing clearly, it freezes. This is called decision fatigue + mental saturation.

The solution isn’t pushing harder. It’s reducing mental load and restoring structure.

Brain Dump

Write every open task down. Chaos becomes manageable when visible.

One Small Win

Complete one small task to regain momentum.

The 3-Step Reset

1. Physical Reset – Drink water, breathe slowly, step outside.
2. Mental Reset – Identify the top 3 real priorities.
3. Structural Reset – Break one big task into 3 micro-actions.
